Parasailing

For those of you for whom a quiet suntan on the beach is not thrilling enough, you can always try your hand at parasailing. You will be rewarded by a breathtaking bird's eye view of the lagoon and its multifarious underwater sea-scapes. Ask the manager of the local boat-house for the update on currents and winds. For additional excitement, choose the "touch and go". On the way down, instead of just reeling back onto the boat, you linger a while above the ocean and then... dunk for a wet, wild, refreshing plunge !!
