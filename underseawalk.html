Undersea walk

Enjoy a safe and fascinating walk on the wet side of Mauritius, to view the undersea world, corals, meeting the fish of our tropical lagoons and much much more. Feel the intense pleasure of a sub-marine stroll... Experience the ultimate sensation where time stops, worries hide away and breathing underwater becomes possible... Also the true experience of feeding fishes bare handed. You will be thrilled. It's a life-time experience !
